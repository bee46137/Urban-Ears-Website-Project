Frequently Asked Questions

Which retailers are your products sold at?
Our products can be found at multiple retailers including: Amazon, Best buy, B&H, Bigeeku, City Super, Hi Department, HIFI+, Imloft, Jingdong Rock Power Station Store, Jingdong Urbanears Flagship Store, Jingdong Zhiyun Audio Store, Jspot and Sound Town.
Do you guys ship internationally?
Yes we do ship internationally! We ship to the following places: Americas, Asia Pacific, Europe and the Middle east.
Is there a pay on delivery option at checkout?
Unfortunately there is no Pay On Delivery (POD) option currently, but we are looking into the possibility of a POD option in the near future. Keep your eyes and ears peeled!
Is there a fast courier option?
Yes there is a fast delivery option but it is only applicable if you are in North America. Some of our retailers do have fast delivery options as well, so double check with a retailer near you.
Are the bluetooth devices compatible with all cellular devices?
Yes all of our bluetooth devices are compatible with all cellular devices as long as the device has bluetooth, you will be good to go!
Will the new Airpods be made from recycled material as well?
Most definitely! All of our electronics are made and will be made from recycled material. It is part of our mission as a company to ensure that we try our best to reduce waste and bring pleasure to our customers.
